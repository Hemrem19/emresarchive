## 5. Migration Guide <a name="migration-guide"></a>

### Free Your Library from Mendeley in 3 Steps

It's easy to move your library to a platform that respects you and your data.

1.  **Export from Mendeley:**
    *   In Mendeley Desktop or Reference Manager, select the papers you want to export.
    *   Go to `File` &rarr; `Export...`.
    *   Choose **RIS - Research Information Systems (*.ris)** as the format and save the file.
    *   View Detailed Guide &rarr;

2.  **Import into citavErs:**
    *   In citavErs, go to `Settings` &rarr; `Import from Zotero/Mendeley`.
    *   Click `Import RIS File` and select the `.ris` file you just saved.

3.  **Done!**
    *   Your papers are now safe in your local citavErs library. Enjoy the freedom, speed, and a truly modern research experience.
